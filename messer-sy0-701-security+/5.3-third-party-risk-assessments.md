# 5.3 Third-Party Risk Assessments

#### Third-Party Risk
- Every organization works with vendors
    - Payroll, customer relationship management, email marketing, travel, raw materials
- Important company data is often shared
    - May be required for cloud-based services
- Perform a risk assessment
    - Categorize risk by vendor and manage the risk
- Use contracts for clear understanding
    - Make sure everyone understands the expectations
    - Use the contract to enforce a secure environment

#### Penetration Testing
- Pentest
- Simulated attack
- Often a compliance mandate, may include a legal requirement
- Regular pentesting by a 3rd-party
    - Very specialized
    - Well-versed experts

#### Rules of Engagement
- Important document
    - Defines purpose and scope
    - Test parameters are defined and known
- Type of testing and schedule
- Rules
    - IP address ranges
    - Emergency contacts
    - Handling sensitive information
    - In-scope and out-of-scope devices or applications

#### Right-to-audit Clauses
- Common to work with business partners
    - Data Sharing
    - Outsourcing
- Third-party providers
    - ISP?
    - Can hold all of your data
    - Is your provider secure?
- Right-to-audit should be in the contract
    - Legal agreement to have the option to perform a security audit at any time
    - Everyone agrees to terms and conditions
    - Ability to verify security before a breach occurs

#### Evidence of Internal Audits
- Evaluate the effectiveness of security controls
    - Have a third-party perform an audit
- May be required for compliance
    - Good idea, even without industry standards
- Check for security controls and processes
    - Access management, offboarding, password security, VPN controls, etc
    - Always an opportunity for improvement
- Perform at a reasonable frequency

#### Supply Chain Analysis
- Systems involved when creating a product
    - Organizations, people, activities, resources
- Process
    - Get a product or service from supplier to customer
    - Evaluate coordination between groups
    - Identify areas of improvement
    - Assess IT systems supporting operation
    - Document business process changes
- SolarWinds March-June 202
    - Announced in December 2020
    - Malware was snuck into valid SolarWinds software with digital signature
    - 18,000 to 300,000 customers potentially impacted

#### Independent Assessments
- Bring in an outsider to evaluate your security and provide recommendations
    - Specialists in their field
    - Can provide options you haven't considered

#### Vendor Selection Process
- Due diligence
    - Check a company before doing business
    - Investigate and verify information
- Conflict of interest
    - Something might compromise judgement
    - Potential vendor also does business with your largest competitor
    - Potential vendor is related to your CFO
    - Bribes

#### Vendor Monitoring
- Ongoing management of vendor relationship
- Reviews should occur on a regular basis
    - Financial health check, IT security reviews, news articles, social media posts
- Different vendors may be checked for different indicators
    - Quant/Qual analysis
- Assign a person to be in charge of vendor relationship
    - Also in charge of monitoring

#### Questionnaires
- Important part of due diligence and monitoring
    - Get answers directly from vendor
- Security-related questions
- Results are used to update a vendor risk analysis
    - Constantly updated
