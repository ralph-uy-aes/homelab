# 4.6 Multifactor Authentication

#### Multifactor Authentication
- Prove who you are
    - Password, mobile app, GPS location
- Factors
    - Something you know, what you have, who you are, and where you are
    - However there are more factors than just this

#### Something You Know
- Password
- PIN or Personal Identification Number
    - ATM
- Pattern locks

#### Something You Have
- Smart Card
    - Debit Card
    - Usually used with a PIN
- USB Security Key
- Hardware or software tokens
    - Generates pseudo-random auth codes
- Your Phone
    - SMS a code to your phone

#### Something You Are
- Biometrics
    - Fingerprint, Retina, Voice
    - Usually stores a mathematical representation of your biometrics
    - Difficult to change
- Typically not standalone, biometrics is not reliable enough

#### Somewhere You Are
- Provide a factor based on your location
- IP address
- GPS
