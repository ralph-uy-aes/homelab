# 5.2 Risk Management Strategies

#### Risk Management Strategies
- Risk Transfer
    - Move risk to another party
    - Purchasing cybersecurity insurance
- Risk Acceptance
    - Business decision
    - Often the usual course of action
- Accept with exemption
    - IF a security policy/regulation cannot be followed
    - May be based on available security controls, size of organization, total assets, etc
    - Exemption may need approval
- Accept with exception
    - Internal security policies are not applies
    - Monthly security updates must be applied within 3 calendar days
    - Monthly updates cause a critical software package to crash
    - Exception is made to the update timeframe
- Avoid
    - Stop participating in high-risk activity
    - Effectively removes risk
- Mitigate
    - Reduce risk level
    - Invest in security systems

#### Risk Reporting
- Formal Document
    - Identifies risks
    - Detailed information per risk
- Usually created for senior management
    - Make decisions regarding resources, budgeting, additional security tasks
- Commonly includes critical and emerging risks
    - Most important considerations
