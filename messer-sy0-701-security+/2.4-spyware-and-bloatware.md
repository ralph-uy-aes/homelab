# 2.4 Spyware and Bloatware

#### Spyware
- Malware that spies on you
    - Advertising, identity theft, affiliate fraud
- Can trick you into installing
    - P2P, fake security software
- Browser monitoring
    - Capture surfing habits
- Keyloggers
    - Capture every keystroke
    - Send your keystrokes back to your attacker

#### Protect against spyware
- Maintain your antivirus, always have latest signatures
- Always know what you're installing
- Where's your backup?
    - You might need it someday
- Run some scans
    - Malwarebytes

#### Bloatware
- New computer or phone
    - Includes OS and important apps
- Also includes applications you didn't expect, and probably don't need
- Apps are installed by manufacturer
    - You don't get a choice
    - Manufacturer usually gets paid to include apps
- Uses storage space
    - May add to resource usage
    - May be slower than expected
    - Could open system to exploits

#### Removing Bloatware
- Identify and Remove
    - May be easier said than done
- Use built-in uninstaller or find the app's uninstaller
