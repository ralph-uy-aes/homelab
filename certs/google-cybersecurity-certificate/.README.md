# Google Cybersecurity Certificate Overview

Welcome to the Google Cybersecurity Certificate! This program prepares you for entry-level roles like cybersecurity analyst, security analyst, or SOC analyst—no prior experience required.

## Why Cybersecurity?

As internet access expands globally, so do threats, risks, and vulnerabilities. Organizations urgently need skilled professionals to protect their data, systems, and people.

## The Nine Courses

The program covers risk mitigation, network security, programming (Python and SQL), incident response, and stakeholder communication. You'll also build portfolio projects to showcase your skills.

1. Foundations of Cybersecurity *(current)*
2. Play It Safe: Manage Security Risks
3. Connect and Protect: Networks and Network Security
4. Tools of the Trade: Linux and SQL
5. Assets, Threats, and Vulnerabilities
6. Sound the Alarm: Detection and Response
7. Automate Cybersecurity Tasks with Python
8. Put It to Work: Prepare for Cybersecurity Jobs
9. Accelerate Your Job Search with AI

## Career Benefits

- Earn a Google Certificate badge to share on LinkedIn.
- Prepare for the CompTIA Security+ exam—complete both to earn a dual credential.

# Course 1: Foundations of Cybersecurity

This first course introduces the eight CISSP security domains, key frameworks and controls, the CIA triad, and common analyst tools.

## Course 1 Modules

- **Module 1** – Explore the cybersecurity field and job responsibilities.
- **Module 2** – Learn how cyber threats evolved and review the eight security domains.
- **Module 3** – Study security frameworks, controls, NIST standards, and ethics.
- **Module 4** – Get introduced to SIEM tools, network analyzers, Python, and SQL.

## Learning Formats

- Video lessons, readings, and hands-on labs.
- Self-review activities, interactive plug-ins, and practice quizzes.
- Graded quizzes (80% passing score required for certification).
- Community forum for peer support.

## Tips for Success

- Follow lessons in order; build on prior knowledge.
- Revisit videos, readings, or activities as needed.
- Bookmark useful resources and use the course glossaries.
- Set a regular study schedule—complete the full program in ~6 months at your own pace.
- No penalties for late assignments; deadlines can be reset.

## Earning the Certificate

Pass all graded assignments in all 9 courses with a score of 80% or higher, and either pay the fee, receive financial aid, or be sponsored by an employer/institution.
