# 5.4 Privacy

#### Privacy Legal Implications
- Constantly evolving set of guidelines
- Local/Regional
    - Government collects a lot data about us
    - State and local governments have privacy limits
- National
    - Privacy laws for everyone in the country
    - HIPAA, online privacy for children under 13, etc
- Global
    - Many countries are working together for privacy

#### GDPR - General Data Protection Regulation
- European Union Regulation
    - Data protection and privacy for individuals in the EU
    - Name, address, photo, email, bank details, posts, networking websites, medical information, IP address
- Controls export of personal data
    - Users can decide where their data goes
    - Can request removal of data from search engines
- Gives "data subjects" control of their personal data
    - A right to be forgotten

#### Data Subject
- Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
- Includes everyone
    - Name, ID, address, genetic makeup, physical characteristics, location, etc
- Laws and regulations
    - Privacy is ideally defined from perspective of data subject

#### Data Responsibilities
- High-level data relationships
    - Organizational responsibilities, not always technical
- Data Owner
    - Accountable for specific data, often a senior officer
    - VP of Sales may own customer relationship data
    - Treasurer may own financial data

#### Data Roles
- Data controller
    - Manages the purposes and means by which personal data is processed
- Data processor
    - Processes data on behalf of data controller
    - Often a third-party or a different group
- Ex. Payroll controller and processor
    - Payroll department (data controller) defines payroll amounts and timeframes
    - Payroll company (data processor) processes payroll and stores employee information
- Data custodian/steward
    - Responsible for data accuracy, privacy, security, legality
    - Works directly with data
    - Associates sensitivity labels to data
    - Ensures compliance
    - Manages access rights
    - Implements security controls

#### Data Inventory and Retention
- What data does your organization store?
- Data inventory
    - Listing of all managed data
    - Owner, update frequency, data format
- Internal use
    - Project collaboration, IT security, data quality checks
- External use
    - Select data to share publicly
    - Follow laws and regulations
