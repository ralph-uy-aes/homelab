# 2.4 On-Path Attacks

#### On-path network attack
- How can an attacker watch you without you knowing
    - Formerly known as MitM
- Redirects your traffic
    - Passes it on to destination, you never know it's been redirected
- ARP Poisoning
    - On-path attack on the local IP subnet
    - ARP has no security

#### On-Path Browser Attack
- What if middleman was on the same computer as the victim
    - Malware or trojan does all of proxy work
    - Formerly known as MitM
- Huge advantages for attackers
    - Relatively easy to proxy unencrypted traffic
    - Everything looks normal to the victim
- Malware in your browser waits for you to login to your bank
    - And cleans you out
